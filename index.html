Biography

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student at School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Maryam Saeedifard. We are investigating novel methodologies of modelling, protection, and high-performance simulation for large-scale cyber-physical systems.

Before moving to Georgia Tech, I obtained my B.E. degree with Honors in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2015. I spent my summers in Amazon Web Services, Oak Ridge National Laboratory and Facebook, working on machine learning modelling, GPU-accelerated simulation, and search/information retrieval, respectively.
